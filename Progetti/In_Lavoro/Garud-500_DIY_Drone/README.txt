# Summary

Garud-500 is a fully 3D Print ready DIY Drone Design kit, specially developed for enthusiasts who dream to build their very first custom drone using 3D Printing. This Garud-500 can be 3D printed in commonly used PLA or ABS material and can be assembled without any hassles. The main frame board i.e. Top & Bottom Plate are designed to bear excessive forces during acrobatic flights while the arms are designed with landing gears to reduce additional connecting weight & prevent damage during landing.

Garud-500 can be 3d Printed with below print specifications:
- Layer Height: 200 - 300 Microns
- Infill: 30% (50% for more strength)
- Infill Pattern: Rectilinear
- Shell Thickness: 0.8mm - 1.2mm
- No Support required
- Rafts Enabled
- Temperature - 210 C PLA | 230 C ABS
