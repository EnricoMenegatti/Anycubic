# Summary

Thanks to peaberry for the original design and Johnnyboyye for his remix.
All I did is fix an aberration that appeared in TinkerCad, and added a cut to allow the extruder cable to be dressed down the beam, under the cover and through the motor mount bracket. This design allows the extruder cable to be hidden from view.
